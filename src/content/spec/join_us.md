## Multimodal Researcher
Focus on multimodal AI models, especially at the intersection of computer graphics and computer vision.

### Requirements:
- Degree in Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, Software Engineering, AI, or related fields.  
- Strong mathematical foundation.  
- Thorough understanding of mainstream model architectures; able to modify them.  
- Proven multi-GPU training experience.  
- Proficient in Python and PyTorch.  
- Hands-on experience in data cleaning, filtering, and synthetic-data generation for multimodal models.
- Can speak Chinese and English.

### We can provide:
- Remote work most of the time  
- Visa support (work visa & PR pathway)

:::note
Overseas applicants are welcome; we provide visa-assistance for qualified candidates.
:::
